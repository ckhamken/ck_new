Project Overview

Purpose of the station is to auto unload empty vials from a packaging tray and present them for feeding into an auto filling machine. A tray of 162 vials (9 rows, 18 per row) is manually loaded onto an infeed conveyor. The tray is indexed toward a locating unit where it is isolated and positioned for pick. An overhead servo gantry shuttles the gripper unit into position to retrieve an entire row of 18 vials per pick and transfers them onto an outfeed chute. The pick and place gantry performs this process across three infeed conveyors. The gripper unit comprises 18 individual grippers mounted to a base plate that also serves as a manifold, distributing air directly to each gripper and eliminating the need for external fittings and air lines between adjacent grippers.

Gripper Design
18 vials per pick — manifold gripper eliminates external lines

The gripper unit was designed with the base plate serving as an air manifold, distributing air directly to each of the 18 individual grippers. This eliminated all external fittings and air lines between adjacent grippers, allowing them to be mounted directly adjacent to each other and matching the vial row spacing exactly.
