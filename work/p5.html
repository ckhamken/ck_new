Project Overview

The manual assembly line consisting of 10 operator stations produces an assembled EV power distribution module. Station operations vary across component installation, wire harness routing, and fastening. Once fully assembled, the workpiece carrier shuttles toward an automatic end-of-line test station. The carrier is positioned and clamped, then connector plugs at various locations are advanced by pneumatic actuators. Once connected, a customer-supplied test unit is initiated to provide power to each connection point and verify positive continuity. OK assemblies are shuttled to a mezzanine holding area. NOK assemblies are marked via their RFID pallet tag and routed to a rework area for manual inspection.
