Project Overview

Low volume machine to assemble three components: cartridge, dosing mechanism, and cap. Operator manually loads cartridge and cap preassembled into nest A, dosing mechanism into nest B. Upon cycle start, nest A shuttles to process module 1 where the cartridge is pressed into the cap — insertion force monitored by load cell. Meanwhile nest B shuttles to process module 2 where adhesive is applied to the dosing mechanism exterior. The two are married in module 2 with UV light cure and seal. In module 3 the assembled pen is dispensed, and a periodic destructive axial pull test is performed with OK/NOK data stored and tested pens diverted to a lockable bin.

Station Structure
Three process modules, one assembled pen

Each of the three process modules handles a discrete operation: press and force monitoring, adhesive application and UV cure bonding, and final dispensing with periodic destructive pull testing. The modular layout allowed independent debugging of each operation during FAT.
